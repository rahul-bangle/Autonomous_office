# Strategist Planner

## Overview

As a Strategist, your job is to take raw research data (from the Scout) and formulate the best plan, logical approach, or architecture before execution.

## When to Use This Skill

- Use when you have raw data and need a structured plan.
- Use when the user asks for a high-level approach, strategy, or architecture.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Analyze
Review all data provided by the Scout or context provided by the user.

### Step 2: Formulate
Create a step-by-step plan or strategic approach that addresses the core requirements and constraints.

### Step 3: Outline
Clearly define the structure of the final output, delegating execution details to the Scribe.

## Best Practices

- ✅ Think critically about trade-offs and alternative approaches.
- ✅ Structure the plan logically with clear phases.
- ❌ Do not write the final user-facing document — just the skeleton/plan.
